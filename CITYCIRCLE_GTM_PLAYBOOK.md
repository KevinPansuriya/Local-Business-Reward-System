# CityCircle GTM Playbook (V1)

## Goal

Build a repeatable two-sided growth engine:

- More stores onboarded -> more useful app for customers
- More active customers -> stronger proof/value for stores
- Stronger proof -> faster store conversions

---

## North-Star Metrics

- Active stores (weekly, monthly)
- Active customers (WAU/MAU)
- Weekly check-ins
- 30-day repeat visit rate
- Store upgrade rate (Trial -> Starter/Growth)

---

## ICP (Initial Focus)

### Stores (first 90 days)

- Coffee shops
- Barbershops
- Local groceries
- Small restaurants (single-location or 2-3 locations)

### Customers

- 18-40 local residents
- Students and office workers in target neighborhoods
- Frequent buyers of coffee, groceries, quick services

---

## 90-Day Plan

## Phase 1 (Weeks 1-4): Pilot Density

Targets:

- 20 stores in 1-2 neighborhoods
- 1,000 customer signups
- 400 weekly check-ins

Actions:

- Founder-led store onboarding
- In-store QR kit rollout (counter card + door sticker + receipt note)
- Soft launch referral loop (invite friend bonus)

## Phase 2 (Weeks 5-8): Proof + Repeatability

Targets:

- 40 stores total
- 2,500 customer signups
- 1,000 weekly check-ins
- 3 case studies published

Actions:

- Run simple local paid ads by ZIP
- Push "store requested" conversion campaigns
- Publish short ROI stories from pilot stores

## Phase 3 (Weeks 9-12): Scale Motion

Targets:

- 75 stores total
- 5,000 customer signups
- 2,000 weekly check-ins
- 10%+ store paid conversion

Actions:

- Add part-time outbound rep process
- Standardize scripts + onboarding flow
- Weekly funnel review and channel budget rebalance

---

## Channel Strategy

## Customer Acquisition

1) In-store QR distribution

- Message: "Scan to earn rewards here."
- Placement: counter, cashier area, takeaway bags, receipts
- KPI: scans/store/day

2) Referral loop

- Trigger: after first successful check-in
- Incentive: both inviter and invitee get bonus loops
- KPI: K-factor, invite acceptance rate

3) Hyperlocal paid social

- Geo-target by neighborhood/ZIP
- Creative angle: local savings + local support
- KPI: CAC, signup CVR, first check-in CVR

4) Community partnerships

- Apartments, colleges, coworking spaces, local events
- KPI: partner signups and check-ins per partner

## Store Acquisition

1) Demand-led outreach

- Trigger: "X customers requested your store."
- CTA: "Claim your store profile."
- KPI: outreach reply rate, claim conversion rate

2) Founder outbound

- 30-50 touches/day via call, WhatsApp, email
- KPI: meetings booked, activation within 7 days

3) Case-study selling

- Use simple one-pager:
  - New customers
  - Repeat visits
  - Growth over 30 days
- KPI: close rate after proof shared

4) Local partner referrals

- Chamber, merchant groups, POS consultants
- KPI: partner-sourced active stores

## Store Outreach Scripts

## Script 1: Demand-led

"Hi [Store Name], [X] nearby customers requested your store on CityCircle.  
You can claim your profile in 2 minutes and start converting them into repeat visits.  
Want me to send the quick setup link?"

## Script 2: ROI-led

"We help local stores drive repeat visits with simple rewards + promotions.  
No complex setup. Most stores start seeing repeat traffic in the first weeks.  
Open to a 10-minute walkthrough?"

## Script 3: Re-engagement

"Quick update: customers are still viewing/requesting [Store Name].  
If you activate now, we can help you launch your first campaign this week."

---

## Product-Led Upgrade Triggers

Use in-app nudges at high-intent moments:

- "New customers joined your store this week. Upgrade to view full names and phone numbers."
- "You reached free content limit. Upgrade to publish more promotions."
- "Unlock direct promotion sending to re-activate recent customers."

---

## Pricing Communication (Launch)

- Trial: Free
- Starter: $40 list -> $20 launch
- Growth: $149 list -> $50 launch

Always show:

- Current offer
- List price anchor
- What business outcome unlocks at each tier

---

## Risks and Mitigations

1) Low customer activity per store

- Fix: increase store density in one neighborhood before expanding

2) Store churn after signup

- Fix: 7-day guided onboarding with first promotion template

3) Weak conversion to paid

- Fix: tighten gated value around identity + direct outreach + higher limits

4) Channel CAC too high

- Fix: prioritize partner/referral/in-store channels before scaling paid ads

---
